Setup OTP

Follow these steps to set up your OTP:

  1. Download and install an OTP app such as Google Authenticator or Authy on your mobile device.
  2. Open the OTP app and select the option to add a new account.
  3. Scan the QR code below with your OTP app.
  4. Once the QR code is scanned, your OTP app will start generating one-time passwords.
  5. Use the generated OTP to log in to your account.

After scanning the QR code, you can log in using the OTP generated by your app.
